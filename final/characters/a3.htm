Marie

マリー

“Mn… good morning.
Or afternoon, or evening…”
Full Name: Marie Aylesbury
Species: Dreamer
Age: 15
Birthday: December 10
Genre: Adventure games
Likes: Sleeping, pajamas
Dislikes: Not sleeping, not pajamas

Introduction

An eternally sleepy girl with an otherworldly aura. She only wears pajamas whether she's indoors or not.

She was one of 20 participants in a secret experiment, which sought to reach other worlds through dreams.

It seemed to be going well at first, but it suddenly went south, and by the time it was over, she and Prinn were the only survivors.

Neither of them minded that much, though. There were more important things to worry about.

What they discovered in those dreams, was something no human could imagine…

Features

Switch between abilities using Memories

The Memories Marie acquired from the dreamscape give her a wide and eclectic range of abilities.

She may look sleepy, but she's a character who excels in clearing stages at a high speed, suited to those who like running around and reacting to things on the spot.

Special A: Umbrella

A reliable umbrella. Surprisingly, it's Marie's signature weapon, but instead of hitting enemies with it, she just splashes them with water. It works, one way or another.

Unfortunately, its range is short, so it's probably better for Marie to avoid enemies altogether.

Special B: Cat Ears

A pair of ears and a tail. Apparently, they're real, and they make Marie move at superhuman speeds.

The problem is the stamina cost. It's not much more effective than running normally, but it's great if you need to get away in a pinch.

Special X: Pajamas

Marie's favorite pajamas. By standing still in them, she quickly regains stamina.

Maybe sleeping on her feet makes her stronger…?

Special Y: Ghost

Marie takes the appearance of a ghost. It looks like a Halloween costume, but as long as Marie's wearing it, it's the real deal.

It causes her to move slower, but in exchange, she can slip past enemies, obstacles, and so on without a problem.

It's almost cheating, really, but you have to watch out for the stamina cost when moving through things.
