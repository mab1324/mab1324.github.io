Auretta Edmiston

アウレッタ・エドミストン

“You also have magic books, right?
I’d like to see them sometime.”
Species: Human
Age: “Young-ish”
Birthday: May 15
Genre: Puzzle games
Likes: Books, magic, Carnelia, people in general
Dislikes: Sad and scary things

Introduction

A girl who fell down a rabbit hole and entered the Land of the Lost, where the souls of discarded items gather.

Since everyone there was hostile to humans, it was near-death experience after near-death experience, but…

“We’re all friends now, so it’s fine!”

Features

Wield the versatile power of magic books

As a magic user, Auretta is an all-around character who's good at everything.

She carries with her a Thunder Tome, a Wind Tome, a Fire Tome, and a Plant Tome. Use them wisely, and you can turn any situation to your advantage.

Special A: Fire Tome

A book containing a spell for hurling fire.

It can be used to attack enemies and destroy obstacles from a distance. With its high rate of fire, spamming it is encouraged.

It's an unusually strong option for someone like Auretta, but maybe that's the difference between an ordinary human and a magic user.

Special B: Wind Tome

A book containing a spell to summon powerful winds.

You can create stationary gusts of wind that send whoever touches them flying in a certain direction. Literally sow the wind and reap the whirlwind.

Use it both to enhance your movement and hinder your opponents.

Special X: Thunder Tome

A book containing a stunning spell.

With this, Auretta can place an invisible trap that stops her opponents for a few seconds. One isn't a big deal, but a bunch of them in one place is a real nuisance.

Luckily for those who have to deal with these things, they're easy to destroy, but you'll also have to pay attention to the other screen to know where they are.

Special Y: Plant Tome

A book containing a spell for instant plant growth.

It forms a tough barrier out of thorny vines. Use it to block passages and shield against enemy attacks.
