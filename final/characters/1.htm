Auretta Edmiston

アウレッタ・エドミストン

“You also have magic books, right?
I’d like to see them sometime.”
Species: Human
Age: “Young-ish”
Birthday: May 15
Genre: Puzzle games
Likes: Books, magic, Carnelia, people in general
Dislikes: Sad and scary things

Introduction

A girl who fell down a rabbit hole and entered the Land of the Lost, where the souls of discarded items gather.

Since everyone there was hostile to humans, it was near-death experience after near-death experience, but…

“We’re all friends now, so it’s fine!”

Features

Wield the versatile power of magic books

As a magic user, Auretta is an all-around character who's good at everything.

She carries with her a Thunder Tome, a Wind Tome, a Fire Tome, and a Plant Tome. Use them wisely, and you can turn any situation to your advantage.

Fire Tome

Hurl fire at enemies or obstacles

Wind Tome

Sow the wind and reap the whirlwind

Thunder Tome

Lay a trap to stun your foes

Plant Tome

Block the way by growing plants
