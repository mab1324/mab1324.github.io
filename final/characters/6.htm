Mirei Awahasu

粟蓮 美怜

“In this world, the one who controls information controls everything.
That’s why I, Mirei, am the world’s strongest yōkai!”
Species: Bake-danuki
Age: 21
Birthday: June 23
Genre: Survival games
Likes: Urban legends, the Internet, storytelling
Dislikes: Other yōkai, Hikari

Introduction

A full-of-herself troublemaker who’s out to prove she’s better than everyone.

Despite being a yōkai, she’s exactly as old as she looks, and she blends in perfectly with modern society. In essence, aside from her spiritual powers, she’s just a 21-year-old college student with a really distorted mindset.

Features

Call forth the yōkai of the modern world

Level 1 Enemy: Kunekune

A wriggly white thing. Looking at it is fine, but identifying it will drive you mad. Don't look at it.

Thanks to their spirits, the heroines aren’t that susceptible to it, but the more there are on-screen at once, the harder it is to tell what’s going on.

Level 2 Enemy: Sukima Onna

A modern yōkai who lurks in the gaps of people’s homes.

She's both strong and hard to see, so your opponents absolutely have to watch out for her.

Level 3 Enemy: Hasshaku-sama

A tall, ghostly woman in a sundress who goes around saying “po po po”.

She sounds silly, and she moves slowly, but her attack is devastating.

Not to mention, getting away from her is a bit harder than it seems…

Boss Enemy: Kotoribako

A puzzle box from occult legend, sometimes described as hatred incarnate.

The details are disturbing, but in short, there’s a curse on it that kills anyone nearby who fulfills certain criteria.

When Mirei uses it, she hides it in a random place near her opponent, forcing them to find and destroy it or take damage indefinitely.

It almost guarantees a large amount of damage on use, so while it may be a very easy to handle Boss, it’s also a very dangerous one.
