Introduction

A strange person who likes hunting for monsters, ghosts, and other things that go bump in the night.

She’s always thought those kinds of beings were fascinating, and when some of her friends decided to break into a haunted house, she gladly came along.

As it turned out, the house really was haunted, and everyone almost died from it — but that didn’t stop her from wanting to visit more of them.

Armed with the mystical knowledge of her ancestors, she came across all kinds of yōkai, and made records of them while overcoming their attacks.

Then, one night, she met the bake-danuki who would become her greatest enemy…
