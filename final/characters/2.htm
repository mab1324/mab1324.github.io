Introduction

The demonic spirit of a discarded rabbit doll who rules over the Land of the Lost, and the main person behind its creation.

It was almost a century ago, when she realized that the spirits of abandoned items, beings who craved the affection of those who once loved them, had no society of their own where they could form new bonds.

With great magic, Carnelia formed a fairyland hidden in the wilderness. It would draw beings without “purpose” towards it, and prevent those who had it from entering.

In time, a society parallel to humanity was born. But for this society to hate humans was inevitable. Carnelia hates them as well.

Still, she knew that in order for the Land of the Lost to thrive, it would need to gain a strong footing in the wider supernatural world, and that meant dealing with humans.

And so, about 95 years later, she was wondering if it’d be best to use force, when an answer appeared in the form of a human girl…
