Overview

Infinite Alice is a player-versus-player split-screen RPG horror game, where two players race to complete stages while interfering with each other's progress.

In this game, the main obstacles are puzzles and the stages themselves, as well as the enemies created by the players' spirits.

There aren't any lives, but beware â€” if you die, in addition to losing progress, you'll get a time penalty.

You win if you complete the stage first, or if your opponent runs out of time before you.

With one of seven(?) pairs of heroine and spirit, climb Castle Phantasmagoria and discover the truth!
