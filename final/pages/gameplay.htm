Gameplay

Overview

Infinite Alice is a player-versus-player split-screen RPG horror game, where two players race to complete floors while interfering with each other's progress.

In this game, the main obstacles are puzzles and the stages themselves, as well as the various enemies created by the players' spirits.

There aren't any lives, and you can die as many times as you like, but beware; aside from losing progress, you'll get a time penalty.

The first person to complete the stage wins. Alternatively, the first player to run out of time loses.

With one of seven(?) pairs of survivor and spirit, scale Castle Phantasmagoria and discover the truth!
