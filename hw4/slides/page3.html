The Game: The Game
  • The Game: The Game is a dating simulator-style visual novel which has the player navigate through a series of confrontations with pick-up artists at a bar.
  • It is the culmination of Angela Washko's research into the pick-up artist/seduction community, reproducing real strategies used and taught by pick-up artists.
  • It was presented as an installation at the Museum of the Moving Image in early 2018.
